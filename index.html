week three

By Yours Truly | March 28 2017

So it's Tuesday, and here we are in the middle of week three! Woot woot! I'm really surprised I've made it so far without any seriously crippling mental breakdowns.

Currently I'm feeling really confident in my HTML and CSS capabilities and I'm also starting to discover that I really enjoy web design and all that other creative jazz. However, this causes some internal struggles cause my creative brain likes to overrun my logical brain and then I end up spending way to much time trying to decide on the perfect shade of mauve, or which serif and sans-serif fonts work best together. On that note, I have found that the internet can be a scary black-hole of information overload if you're not careful. So future me, or anyone else reading this (are other people really going to read this......??) YOU HAVE BEEN WARNED.

Week three has also meant delving further into the infamous JavaScript.. which is still somewhat of an enigma to me. At times I feel like a total JavaScript master and at other times I want to cry silent tears into my keyboard.
